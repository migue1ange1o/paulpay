ferret.cash

A non-custodial streamer and viewer donation messaging system for crypto.

How Ferret Works

Sign up for ferret and paste your crypto address into the user panel.

Generate a special "view-only" monero wallet and upload the wallet.

All that's left to do is to set up OBS and stream on your platform of choice!

Keep Your Coins

Due to Ferret's non-custodial design, you have no need to worry about losing your crypto.

Ferret never touches any of your money and simply acts as a terminal for messages.

Your Streams, Your Business

Everybody is invited to use ferret. We are agnostic about beliefs or intentions and believe that technology is like a shovel, a tool for anybody to use.

Shovel companies don't take away your shovel if they don't like the holes you dig, we won't take away your account if you are a person we dislike.

What you believe or do is none of our business.
